Fundamental q-hypergeometric summation identities

An identity of Euler

If \(|z|<1 \) and (\ |q|<1 \), \[ \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{z^n q^{n(n-1)/2}{(q;q)_n} = (-z;q)_\infty.\]
