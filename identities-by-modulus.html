Rogers—Ramanujan type identities

Identities by their product representations

This page is a catalog of known Rogers-Ramanujan-Slater identities, sorted by their product representations. Use the search bar on the left to jump to a particular modulus. Click on any product to see the series representations.

For help with notation, see the definition of the \(q\)-Pochhammer symbol and of Ramanujan's theta functions.

\(\displaystyle \frac{f(-q,-q^3)}{f(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q;q^2)_\infty^2}\)

Mod 4

\(\displaystyle \frac{f(-q,-q)}{\psi(-q)} = (q,q^2,q^3; q^4)_\infty\)
\(\displaystyle \frac{f(-q)}{\varphi(-q^2)} = \frac{(q,q^3; q^4)_\infty}{(q^2; q^4)_\infty}\)
\(\displaystyle \frac{f(-q^2, -q^2)}{\psi(-q)} = \frac{(q^2;q^4)_\infty^2}{(q,q^3;q^4)_\infty}\)
\(\displaystyle \frac{f(-q,-q^3)}{\varphi(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q,q^2,q^3; q^4)_\infty}\)
\(\displaystyle \frac{f(-q^2,-q^2)}{\varphi(-q)} = \frac{(q^2;q^4)_\infty}{(q,q^3;q^4)_\infty^2} \)

Mod 5

\(\displaystyle G(q) = \frac{f(-q^2,-q^3)}{f(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q, q^4; q^5)_\infty} = \prod_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{(1-q^{5n-1})(1-q^{5n-4})}\)
\(\displaystyle H(q) = \frac{f(-q,-q^4)}{f(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q^2, q^3; q^5)_\infty} = \prod_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{(1-q^{5n-2})(1-q^{5n-3})}\)
