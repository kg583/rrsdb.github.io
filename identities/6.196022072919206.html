Rogers–Ramanujan type identities

Article for infinite product with signature 6:(2,1,0,1,2,0)

\[ F(q) := \sum_{n=0}^\infty r(n)q^n := \frac{f(q,q^2)}{f(-q)} = \frac{\phi(-q^3)}{\phi(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q,q^5;q^6)_\infty^2 (q^2,q^4;q^6)_\infty} \]

Combinatorial interpretation of product

\(r(n)\) is the number of overpartitions of \(n\) where no part is a multiple of 3.

Asymptotic formula for \(r(n)\)

\(\displaystyle r(n) \sim \frac{e^{\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} \pi \sqrt{n}}}{2 \sqrt[4]{2} \ 3^{3/4} n^{3/4}}\) as \( n\to\infty. \)
