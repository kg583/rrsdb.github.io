Power series factorization tool

This tool attempts to factor a truncated power series in the form \[ 1 + \sum_{n=1}^T r(n)q^n = \prod_{n=1}^T (1-q^n)^{-a_n}.\] where equality holds for the coefficients only up to \(q^T\). This is useful for finding patterns in order to conjecture identities of the form \[ 1 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty r(n)q^n = \prod_{n=1}^\infty (1-q^n)^{-a_n}.\]
